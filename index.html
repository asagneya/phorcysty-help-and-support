About Phorcysty

Phorcysty is an AI-powered color transfer application that allows you to apply color styles between images and videos using server-side processing. The app is designed to make color styling simple, fast, and accessible with just a single click.

Phorcysty supports multiple workflows, including:

  • Image to Image color transfer
  • Video to Video color transfer
  • Image to Video color transfer
  • Video to Image color transfer

In addition to color style transfer, Phorcysty also provides color enhancement tools that allow you to adjust and refine your results, such as Brightness, Contrast, Saturation, Hue, Temperature, and Tint.

All processing is performed through secure AI-powered systems to deliver consistent results while preserving the original structure and visual quality of your media.

How to Use Phorcysty

Select a source image or video from your device (from which the color style will be taken).

Select the target image or video (on which the color style will be applied).

Tap the Process button to start AI-powered color transfer.

Wait while your media is securely processed on the server.

After processing, you can fine-tune the result using the color enhancement tools such as:

  • Brightness
  • Contrast
  • Saturation
  • Hue
  • Temperature
  • Tint

Use the Undo and Redo buttons to revert or reapply changes while editing.

Once you are satisfied with the result, tap Download to save the final output to your device.

Internet Requirement

Phorcysty uses AI-powered server-side processing to deliver high-quality color transfer, color style transformation, and color enhancement results. An active internet connection is required to upload selected media files to the server and receive the processed output.

AI-based features of the app cannot function without an internet connection.

Permissions

Phorcysty requires access to your device’s media storage so that you can select images and videos for color transfer and editing.

Internet permission is required to securely upload selected media files for AI-powered server-side processing and to receive the processed results.

Saving Results

Once processing is complete, you can download the final image or video directly to your device using the Download button.

Downloaded files are saved to your device’s local storage and are accessible from your gallery or file manager. The app does not automatically share your media with any third party.

You have full control over your downloaded content, and you may delete it from your device at any time.

Common Issues & Troubleshooting

  • Processing takes more time than usual
    Processing time depends on file size, video length, and your internet speed. Larger files or longer videos may take a few extra seconds.
  • Slow or no response from the app
    Please make sure you have a stable and active internet connection. Server-side AI processing requires a good network connection.
  • Large images take time to load
    High-resolution images may take a moment to load on the editing screen. This is normal behavior.
  • Download button not responding
    Wait until processing is fully completed. Try again once the result is displayed.
  • App feels slow or laggy
    Close other background apps and ensure your device has enough free storage and memory.
  • Output quality not as expected
    Results depend on the quality, lighting, and colors of the source and target media. Try using clearer images or videos for better results.
  • Video processing takes longer than images
    Videos are larger in size and require more server resources, so they naturally take more time to process.

If the issue continues, please restart the app and check your internet connection. For further assistance, you can always contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Why does video processing take more time than images?
A: Videos are larger in size and contain multiple frames, so they require more server resources and time for AI processing.

Q: Where is my processed image or video saved?
A: The final output is downloaded directly to your device’s local storage and can be found in your gallery or file manager.

Q: Can I use Phorcysty without an internet connection?
A: No. An active internet connection is required for AI-powered server-side processing features.

Q: Are my images and videos stored on your servers?
A: No. Your media files are used only for temporary processing and are not permanently stored on our servers.

Q: Are my images or videos used to train your AI models?
A: No. Your personal media files are never used to train, improve, or refine our AI models.

Q: Does Phorcysty share my media with third parties?
A: No. Your images and videos are not shared with any third parties. Only limited technical data may be collected by services like Google AdMob and Firebase for ads and app stability, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Q: Why does processing sometimes fail or take longer?
A: Processing time may vary based on file size, video length, server load, and your internet speed. A slow or unstable connection can affect performance.

Q: Can I undo or redo my changes?
A: Yes. You can use the Undo and Redo buttons while editing to revert or reapply changes.

Q: What formats are supported?
A: Phorcysty supports common image and video formats supported by your device. Availability may vary based on device and system compatibility.
